Do I need to take an appointment before visiting?

Yes, it is advisable to fix an appointment by phone or WhatsApp before visiting, so that sufficient time can be reserved for discussion of your matter.

Carry all documents related to your matter ‚Äì agreements, notices, FIR, court papers, cheques, bank memos, property papers etc. Even rough copies are useful for the first review.
